Pipeline Choice

The Wang et al (2014) article used a number of different pipelines in the analysis of the RNA-Seq and Microarray data. Of these, the P5 analysis pipeline was chosen for our RNA-Seq analysis. The included aspects of the tuxedo package, TopHat, Bowtie and Cufflinks with the additional CummeRbund R package. This commonly used pipeline was preferred due to a reported 2nd highest level of genes found and highest Microarray-RNA-Seq concurrence within the article.
